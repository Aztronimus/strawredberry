De Broglie's mass waves

From relativity we know the energy of a photon is related with its momentum by the following expression: \begin{align} E &= pc\\ \end{align} And from the photoelectric effect experiment we also know that the energy of a photon is given by the expression: \begin{align} E &= hf\\ \end{align} Lastly the following expression relates the wavelength, frequencty and speed of a light wave: \begin{align} \lambda f &= c\\ \end{align} From these 3 equations is possible to get the wavelength of a mass wave \begin{align} pc = hf\\ pc = \frac{hc}{\lambda}\\ \\ pc = hf\\ p = \frac{h}{\lambda}\\ \end{align}
